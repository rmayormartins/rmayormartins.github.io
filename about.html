About me

I am M.Sc. in Telecommunications from Instituto Nacional de Telecomunicações (INATEL) in 2014. Specialist in Telecommunications Systems in 2015. Specialist in Computer Networks 2017. Professional MBA in Systems and Telecommunications Analysis in 2017. Specialist in Systems Engineering in 2018. Electrical Engineer with emphasis on Telematics, from the [Universidade do Sul de Santa Catarina (UNISUL) in 2010.

Since 2015, i'm hold the position of Professor of Basic, Technical and Technological Education, in the area of Telecommunications,   at Instituto Federal de Santa Catarina, Campus São José.
